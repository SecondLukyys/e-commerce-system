To start neo4j and springboot app in vps run:

nohup neo4j console &
nohup mvn spring-boot:run > /dev/null 2>&1 &         mvn spring-boot:run  

To stop neo4j and springboot app in vps run:

ps aux | grep neo4j
kill <PID>

ps aux | grep spring-boot
kill <PID>
